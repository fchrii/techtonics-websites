## Overview

A little highlight from our class, girls - boys.

Students of Grade XI TKJ - 1 are Computer and Network Engineering (TKJ) students in their second year of vocational high school (SMK). They focus on learning various aspects of computer networking, hardware, system security, and basic programming.

## Things

- **Proficient in basic networking**: They understand subnetting, router & switch configurations, and network protocols.
- **Skilled in computers**: They can disassemble, assemble, and troubleshoot computer hardware.
- **Enjoy Children**: Our students enjoy and are united.

## What the teacher said to our class

Typically, students in XI TKJ - 1 start participating in IT competitions, internships, or small projects like setting up LAN networks at school. They are also known for being tech-savvy, discussing technology trends, and often being the go-to tech support for friends from other majors.
